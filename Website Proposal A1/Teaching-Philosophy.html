My Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy is aimed at providing students with a real world focus on all of their content and activities that they engage in.

My activities are focused around the PBL (Project Based Learning) pedagogical framework that provides students with real applications of their newly acquired knowledge

These 3 activities are designed for students gain an understanding of developing a product from a basic starting point and creating that into a fully fleshed out high quality end design.

  2. Activity Two: Design Solutions - Prototype Modelling
    This activity is the continuation of activity 1, this activity is designed as a beginner friendly, introductory activity into the basics of CAD modelling using Autodesk Fusion360 and design choices and theory testing.


  3. Activity Three: Design Solutions - Infographic Advertisement
    This activity is the conclusion of activity 1 and activity 2, this activity is designed as a method for students to experiment with design philosophy to create an infographic that showcases all of student created components
