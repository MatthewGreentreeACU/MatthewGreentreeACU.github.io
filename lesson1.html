Activity Name: Design Solutions - Sketching

  • This task is designed for preliminary Stage 6

  • allocated: 50mins
This activity is an introductory lesson for students to create a prototype model sketch of an existing technology product of their choosing.
This sketch will be used in Activity 2, to create a Autodesk Fusion360 CAD Model.


Outcomes Assessed:

P3.1 investigates and experiments with techniques in creative and collaborative approaches in designing and producing

P4.3 evaluates the processes and outcomes of designing and producing

P5.3 uses a variety of research methods to inform the development and modification of design ideas

Task Instructions

  • Time allocated: 5mins
    •
    You will begin the lesson by selecting a technology company from the links in the resources section below.

  • Time allocated: 10mins
    •  Once the you have selected your company of choice, you will fill out the scaffold sheet found in the "Scaffold" section below, this document will include information such as; what company has been chosen, what product have you decided to create, and why did you choose to create this product.

  • Time allocated: 30mins
    •  ​Once you have completed the scaffold document, you will then begin creating your sketches, you will make 3 variations of the one product as it will give a lot of choice in deciding what sketch will be converted into the Autodesk Fusion360 CAD Model in Activity 2.

  • Time allocated: 5mins
    •  ​In the last 5 minutes of this activity, you will upload your scaffolding document to Google Classroom. By this point in the activity, you should have completed your three sketches (or gotten close to), you will keep the sketches as you will need them for Activity 2 and if you didn't complete all three sketches, you will finish the remaining work at home.
