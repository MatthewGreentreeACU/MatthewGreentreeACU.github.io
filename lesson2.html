Activity Name: Design Solutions - Prototype Modelling

  • This task is designed for preliminary Stage 6

  • allocated: 50mins
This activity is the continuation to activity 1, in this activity you will choose one out of your three sketches to create into a CAD model.
This CAD model will be used for activity 3.


Outcomes Assessed:

P1.1 examines design theory and practice, and considers the factors affecting designing and producing in design projects

P3.1 investigates and experiments with techniques in creative and collaborative approaches in designing and producing

P4.1 uses design processes in the development and production of design solutions to meet identified needs and opportunities

P4.3 evaluates the processes and outcomes of designing and producing

P5.3 uses a variety of research methods to inform the development and modification of design ideas

P6.2 evaluates and uses computer-based technologies in designing and producing

Task Instructions

  • Time allocated: 5mins
    •
    You will decide which out of your three sketches will be the final product that you will be creating a CAD version of.

  • Time allocated: 40mins
    •  Once you have decided which sketch to contextualise into a Autodesk Fusion360 prototype model, you will spend the bulk of this activity in creating your model, if you haven't completed the model in 40mins you will be required to finish the model in your own time. In the resources section below you will find a YouTube playlist with tutorial videos on Autodesk Fusion360, and in the same resource section you will also find written tutorials!
